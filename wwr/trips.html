Kings Valley

Located in Vermont. Openings on April 31st

Kings Valley, a majestic waterway located in the heart of Vermont's lush landscapes, is renowned for its exhilarating rafting experiences. This river carves its way through Kings Valley Park, offering a blend of tranquil stretches and heart-pumping rapids, making it a must-visit destination for both novice and veteran rafters alike.

Death River

Located in Nevada. Openings on May 6th!

Death River, named for its intense and thrilling rapids, courses through the rugged terrains of Francisco Nevada State Park. This formidable river has become a legendary spot for seasoned white-water rafters seeking the ultimate challenge. Its fierce currents and sudden drops create an adrenaline-fueled experience that has earned it a place in the heart of extreme outdoor enthusiasts.

Sweet River

Located in Utah. Openings on May 25th!

Sweet River, a hidden gem nestled in the serene landscapes of Swiss State Park in Utah, offers a markedly different rafting experience from its more tumultuous counterparts. Known for its gentle currents and picturesque scenery, Sweet River is the ideal destination for families and those new to the sport of rafting.
